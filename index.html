About Me

Mark Bullard was born in Fayetteville, NC in 1983 to parents Brenda and Kenneth Bullard. He has one sibling, Ray Bullard, who is three years older than him. Both brothers are US Army Veterans who were deployed at the same time during OIF/OEF (Operation Iraqi Freedome/Operation Enduring Freedom). Mark entered the service when he was 17 years old, in 2001. He completed his service in 2009, prior starting his educational path in Instructional Design and Technology. He holds a BS, achieved through Walden University, in Instructional Design and Technology - with a concentration in Human Factors and Interface Design (completed 2013). He spent many years, after his service working in various fields using some of the skills taught to him in school but his desire was always to become a web developer.

Mark Bullard struggled to find his way into the IT field, at first, but was given an opportunity in his current position as a QA Tester. Since then, he has learned a variance of abilities in such a short time, in order to apply it to his work and start a foundation of learning. Enter: UNC Coding Bootcamp. He scrounged up the money to pay for the tuition and put his trust in the investment of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer.

He faces his truest challenge, as he embarks on this journey to acheive a dream that has long been just that. With the skills he learns here, Mark will update this webpage as it goes along, adding his achievements in his portfolio.
